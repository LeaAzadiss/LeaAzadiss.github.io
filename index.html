Rainbow Crepe Cake

What is Rainbow Crepe cake??

Rainbow Crepe Cake is a layered cake with a blend of rainbow colors. Rainbow Crepe Cake is currently being much loved by the public, especially for those who like cake. In addition to having a good taste, the look of Rainbow Crepe Cake itself is very beautiful, with rainbow colors. This cake can also be an inspiration for birthday cakes, simply decorate the edges of the cake with cream and toping that you like.

The different types of toppings and crepes stuffing are very diverse. But behind his fame, do you know about the history of crepes?

History of crepes

When viewed from its history, crepes age is very old. According to some sources, crepes were known thousands of years ago. In ancient times people have enjoyed crepes in various forms. Crepes are known as pancakes cooked over a flat frying pan. Europe claims to be the birthplace of crepes. While a text from Celtic, Ireland mentions the habit of people there eating crepes since the 18th century.
Some sources say that crepes were first discovered in France in 1390. Indeed, there are many theories that mention the history of crepes, even with various recipes. But in practice the process of making crepes is not as easy as it has been written, you need time to learn to make crepes.

Ingredients

You need:

  • 5 eggs
  • 65 g sugar
  • 1 g salt
  • 5 g vanilla extract
  • 250 g cake flour
  • 625 g milk
  • 90 g Melted unsalted butter
  • Food coloring (orange, yellow, green, blue)
  • Whipped cream (500g whipping cream + 50g sugar)

Materials

You need:

  • Pan/Teflon
  • Stove
  • Bowl
  • Whisk
  • Measuring cup
  • Filter
  • Spatula
Steps

How's the step??

  1. Add sugar, salt, vanilla extract to the egg and mix it well
  2. Sift the flour and add to the mixture
  3. Add just a little of the milk (125 g) and mix until the flour is completely mixed and Then add all the remaining milk (500 g) and stir well
  4. Add melted unsalted butter and mix
  5. Filter the batter, then cover it and put it in the fridge for about 1 hour
  6. Mix the batter again when you get it out from the fridge and divide it into 4 bowls
  7. Add a little color to each bowl and stir well
  8. Heat the pan, then apply it with a little butter to make 6-7 crepes per color
  9. Put one chilled crepe and put the cream on top, do it over and over
  10. Add the toppings according to your taste
  11. The crepe cake is ready to serve

Make sure before creating the script you have prepared the tools and materials.

Preparing tools and ingredients before cooking, it will make it easier for us when cooking later. So I highly recommend always preparing everything before it starts.

Although it looks simple, making crepe cakes requires skill so pay attention to the tutorial below well and often practice making crepe cakes.
